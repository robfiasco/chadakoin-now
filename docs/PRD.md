# Product Requirements Document — Chadakoin Now

## Summary

Chadakoin Now is a free, Android-first resident utility app for Jamestown, NY. It aggregates city service information — recycling schedules, parking rules, weather, alerts, and local events — into a single, fast, readable interface.

No login. No ads. No noise.

---

## User Problems

**1. Garbage and recycling confusion**
Residents don't know what gets picked up this week. Holiday delays cause missed pickups. The BPU schedule is buried on a website that is hard to navigate on mobile.

**2. Parking rule confusion**
Alternate-side parking rules in Jamestown are date-based (odd/even). Snow emergencies override them. Residents get tickets because they can't easily check what applies today.

**3. No central information hub**
City alerts, events, and service updates are scattered across Facebook, the city website, the BPU site, and local news. There is no single place for a resident to check.

---

## MVP Scope (v0.1.0 – v0.2.0)

The MVP answers 6 core resident questions:

1. Is there a garbage/recycling delay this week?
2. What is getting recycled this week?
3. Which side of the street do I park on today?
4. Are there any active city alerts?
5. What is the weather locally?
6. What city events are coming up?

**Screens included:**
- Home (summary dashboard)
- Recycling (pickup schedule + rotation)
- Parking (today's side + weekly schedule)
- Alerts (status + recent updates)
- Events (monthly event list with filters)

---

## Non-Goals

- This is not a weather app. Weather is a supporting utility only.
- This is not a city portal. No account, no billing, no permits.
- This is not a news app. No editorial content, no comment sections.
- This is not a push notification platform (until Phase 3).

---

## Success Criteria

- Residents can answer the 6 core questions with one glance at the Home screen.
- The app loads in under 2 seconds on a mid-range Android device.
- The recycling and parking screens require zero explanation.
- Alert state is immediately obvious — no scanning required.

---

## Users

Primary: Jamestown, NY residents, all ages, Android-first.
Secondary: Property managers, landlords with multiple addresses.
