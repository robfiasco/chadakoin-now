# v1.1.0 — April 28, 2026

## What's New

### Content
- **Lucille Ball Desi Arnaz Museum** added to the Visit tab under Explore — long overdue for Jamestown's most famous attraction
- **School delays & closings** now surface as a banner on the Home screen when detected in local news feeds, active September through June
- **Gas prices** card on Home showing your region vs. the US national average, with a link to find the cheapest stations nearby via GasBuddy (powered by EIA weekly data)

### Visit tab
- **Open Now filter** — tap the clock icon next to the search bar to filter places to only those currently open
- Improved subtitle and filter labels (Eat · Drink · Stay · Explore)

### Alerts tab
- **Utility resources** section added — quick links to the NYSEG Outage Map and Jamestown BPU

### Sports
- Removed pulsing dot from Next Up / Live Now header

### App
- Loading screen now randomly shows one of the app's built-in themes on each launch
- New **Clarity** high-contrast theme added in Settings (black background, white accents)
- Several unused legacy components and files removed; codebase cleanup

## Infrastructure
- Feedback emails now route through `feedback@chadakoindigital.com`
- Cloudflare Email Routing active for `rob@chadakoindigital.com`
